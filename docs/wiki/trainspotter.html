Gonçalo Pinto, "A importância da sinalética num sistema de transporte de massas"

Author explains why the current network map of the Metro system in Porto is wrong (and misleading) about one line, and how, because of that, users make unnecessary transfers. In short, the current map suggests that one of line F's end is Senhora da Hora, while in practice it goes further, along line A, and stopping at Senhor de Matosinhos.
